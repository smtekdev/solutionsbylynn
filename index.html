How can I donate to Solutions by Lynn?

Donating to Solutions by Lynn is easy. You can make an online donation, drop off items at our location, participate in fundraising events, etc.

What types of items do you accept for donations?

We accept a variety of items, including baby clothes, diapers, bedding, eyeglasses, coats, school supplies, etc.

Can I volunteer with Solutions by Lynn

Yes, we welcome volunteers who are passionate about our mission. You can help sort donations, assist at events, provide mentorship, etc.

How does Solutions by Lynn support adopted school students?

We support adopted school students by providing eyeglasses, coats, and school supplies, e.g., partnering with local schools, identifying students in need, etc. Our goal is to ensure every student has the tools for success.
