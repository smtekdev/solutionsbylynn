About Us

Welcome to Solutions by Lynn

Where we take problems and turn them into Solutions. We are a group of highly trained professionals in human services who came together to close gaps that pregnant and parenting teenagers and elementary school children attending Title-1 schools living in Wake County, North Carolina face. These gaps that needy families with newborns and minor children experience are ones the political arena seems oblivious to. We don’t think of our services as duplicates of what is already happening but as an addition. We want to ensure that babies and elementary school children are comfortable, safe, and thriving by meeting their need for clothing. And we are dedicated to serving regardless of race, religion, moral standards, or political affiliation. As we embrace all and pray for change.

Discover More
Our Mission

Solutions by Lynn provides indigent pregnant and parenting teens with baby essentials. In October and March we provide students attending a selected Title-1 school with new athletic shoes and other necessities. And we teach a character and behavior curriculum to children ages 7-17.
